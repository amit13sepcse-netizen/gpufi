Executive Summary

üéØ Key Achievement

GPU acceleration with PG-Strom demonstrates significant performance improvements for analytical workloads on large datasets. Testing on a 1 billion row table (bench_numeric_xxlarge) shows up to 2.07x speedup for filtering operations and 1.96x speedup for complex mathematical computations.

Key Findings

  • GpuScan offloading is functional: Queries with predicates and projections successfully offload to GPU
  • Best performance on compute-heavy queries: Mathematical operations and filtering see the most benefit
  • Plain SELECT * remains CPU-bound: No offload for simple scans without computation
  • GpuPreAgg requires full extension: Pre-aggregation blocked by missing helper functions in minimal setup
  • Production-ready for scan/filter workloads: Stable 2x improvements on billion-row analytics
